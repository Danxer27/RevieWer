The paper proposes a balanced convergence of HATPRINT and AIPRINT – a symbiotic model where AI handles routine tasks, while human reviewers retain critical judgment and ethical oversight. The manuscript highlights the challenges of managing this transition, emphasizing the need for proactive governance and ethical frameworks.
